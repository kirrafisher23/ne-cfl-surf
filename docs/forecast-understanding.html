Forecast Background

Enjoy separate forecast data from Surf Captain.

Winds

Offshore (land → ocean): clean, glassy waves; strong winds (+9 kts) makes paddling into the wave harder.
Onshore (ocean → land): common in Florida, creates wind swells; fun but can get choppy OR blown out.
Cross/Side-shore: mixed bag of conditions depends on the spots wind protection; expect a current.

Swell Periods

Swell period = time between waves (seconds).
10s+ = ground or hurricane swell.
5–6s + onshore winds = wind swell (short, choppy but surfable).
less then 5s = dont bother.
